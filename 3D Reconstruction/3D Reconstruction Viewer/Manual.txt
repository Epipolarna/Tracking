Reads files of the type 'alx' and they should be formated as <name><iteration>.<minor>.alx
where <iteration> is an integer greater than 0 and minor is 1 or 2 (1 = before BA, 2 = after BA)

Navigation:
	Next file:  	* Mouse right click
					* Right keyboard arrow
	Previous file:  * Mouse left click
					* Left keyboard arrow
	Inc. scale:  	* Mouse xbutton1 click
					* addition-key on keyboard
	Dec. scale:  	* Mouse xbutton2 click
					* subtraction-key on keyboard
	Reload file:	* F5
